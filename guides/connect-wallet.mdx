ACTFUN runs on Arc Testnet, a network you need to add manually to MetaMask before you can interact with the app. This guide walks you through adding the network, connecting your wallet on [actfun.](https://actfun.fun)xyz , and funding it with testnet ARC.

<Note>
   ACTFUN currently supports MetaMask only. Browser extension wallets that inject a generic `window.ethereum` provider may work, but are not officially supported. Mobile wallets and WalletConnect are not available.
</Note>

## Add Arc Testnet to MetaMask

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open MetaMask and go to network settings">
    Click the network selector at the top of the MetaMask popup (it usually shows **Ethereum Mainnet**). Then click **Add network** at the bottom of the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to manual entry">
    On the Add a network screen, click **Add a network manually** to open the custom network form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the Arc Testnet details">
    Enter the following values exactly:

    | Field | Value |
    | --- | --- |
    | Network name | `Arc Testnet` |
    | New RPC URL | `https://rpc.testnet.arc.network` |
    | Chain ID | `5042002` |
    | Currency symbol | `ARC` |
    | Block explorer URL | `https://testnet.arcscan.app` |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and switch">
    Click **Save**. MetaMask will prompt you to switch to Arc Testnet — click **Switch to Arc Testnet** to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect to ACTFUN

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Navigate to [actfun.](https://actfun.fun)xyz  in the same browser where MetaMask is installed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Wallet">
    Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top-right corner of the page. MetaMask will open and ask you to select an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve the connection">
    Select the account you want to use and click **Connect**. The button in the app will update to show your shortened wallet address, confirming you're connected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm you're on Arc Testnet">
    If MetaMask is on the wrong network, the app will show a warning. Switch to Arc Testnet in MetaMask to dismiss it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Get ARC from the faucet

Every transaction on Arc Testnet  mining, trading, deploying a token  costs a small amount of ARC for gas. Use the faucet to get free testnet ARC.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the faucet">
    Open [faucet.testnet.arc.network](https://faucet.testnet.arc.network).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your wallet address">
    Paste your wallet address into the input field. You can copy it from MetaMask by clicking your account name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request ARC">
    Click the **Request** (or **Drip**) button. The faucet will send testnet ARC to your wallet within a few seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm receipt">
    Open MetaMask and check your balance. You should see ARC in your account. If you need more, wait for the faucet's cooldown period and request again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Every action on  ACTFUN mining a token, buying, selling, launching, or claiming a refund requires ARC to pay for gas, plus any mining fee set by the token creator. Keep a comfortable ARC balance in your wallet so transactions don't fail unexpectedly.
</Tip>
