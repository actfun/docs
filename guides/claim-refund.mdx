title: "How to claim back USDC mining fees before graduation"

Every time you mine a token you pay an USDC fee, and that fee accumulates in the launcher contract. Before the token graduates, you can claim back the full amount you have paid at any time. This is a safety valve that lets you recover your USDC if a token's mining phase stalls or you simply change your mind.

## When a refund is available

A claimable refund appears on the token's detail page when both of the following are true:

1. The token **has not yet graduated** (less than 95% mined).
2. Your wallet has paid at least one ARC mining fee on that token.

If the token has already graduated, refunds are no longer available — the accumulated fees are locked into the AMM liquidity pool at the moment of graduation.

<Warning>
  Once a token graduates, all collected USDC fees are permanently seeded into the liquidity pool. There is no way to claim a refund after graduation. If you want your USDC back, claim it before the final mine.
</Warning>

## Claim your refund

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the token's detail page">
    Go to [actfun.xyz](https://actfun.fun) , find the token you mined, and click its card to open the detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the refund panel">
    On the right side of the page, look for the **Claim USDC Refund** panel. It shows your claimable USDC balance — the total of all fees you have paid on this token that have not yet been refunded.

    If the panel is not visible, either the token has already graduated or your wallet has no unclaimed fees on this token.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Claim Refund">
    Click the **Claim Refund** button. MetaMask opens a transaction confirmation. This transaction has no ETH/ARC value — it just calls the contract to transfer your accumulated fees back to your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm in MetaMask">
    Click **Confirm**. The contract clears your fee balance to zero and transfers the full claimable amount to your wallet in one call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify receipt">
    After the transaction confirms, the panel shows a **Refund claimed successfully!** message. Check your MetaMask balance to confirm the USDC has arrived. The refund panel disappears because your claimable balance is now zero.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Claiming a refund does not affect your mined tokens. Tokens you received from previous mines remain in your wallet regardless of whether you claim a refund. The refund only returns the USDC fees — not the tokens.
</Note>

## What happens if you mine again after claiming

If you claim a refund and then mine the same token again before it graduates, a new claimable balance starts accumulating from your subsequent mines. You can claim again at any point, as many times as you like, as long as the token hasn't graduated.
