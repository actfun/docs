Anyone can deploy a token, the community mines 95% of the supply by writing funny posts, and once fully mined the token automatically opens for live trading on its own built-in DEX. No backend, no admin keys, no external exchange needed.

## How MINEPAD works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a token">
    Any wallet can deploy a new token via the LaunchpadFactory. Set the name, symbol, total supply, mine amount per call, cooldown period, and ARC fee per mine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Community mines it">
    Other users mine the token by writing a funny post and paying a small ARC fee. Each mine mints tokens directly to the miner's wallet. Per-wallet cooldowns and daily caps prevent spam.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Token graduates">
    When 95% of the supply has been mined, the contract automatically graduates — minting 5% as LP reserve tokens and opening a constant-product AMM seeded with those tokens plus all accumulated ARC fees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trade on the built-in DEX">
    Buy and sell the token using the AMM built into the same contract. No external DEX, no bridging, no extra setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>
