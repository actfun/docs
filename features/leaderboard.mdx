ACTFUN tracks on-chain mining activity across every token and surfaces two community recognition features: the **Global Leaderboard** and the **Hall of Fame**.

## Global Leaderboard

The Global Leaderboard is a live ranking of every wallet address that has mined at least one ACTFUN token. Rankings are based on total tokens mined across all ACTFUN tokens — not just a single token.

<Note>
  Rankings update in near real time as new mines are confirmed on-chain, powered by the Goldsky Turbo event pipeline feeding the live activity index.
</Note>

### What the leaderboard shows

| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Rank | Position on the all-time leaderboard |
| Wallet | Shortened wallet address (click to view on Arcscan) |
| Total mined | Cumulative tokens mined across all ACTFUN tokens |
| Tokens mined | Number of distinct tokens the wallet has mined |
| Last active | Timestamp of the most recent mine transaction |

### How to reach the top

The leaderboard rewards volume and consistency. To climb the ranks:

- Mine tokens early and often, within your per-wallet cooldown and daily cap limits.
- Spread activity across multiple tokens to increase your total count.
- Every mine is on-chain and permanent — there is no way to game or reverse your history.

## Hall of Fame

The Hall of Fame is a permanent record of every ACTFUN token that successfully graduated. A token enters the Hall of Fame the moment its graduation transaction confirms on-chain — when 95% of the mineable supply has been mined.

### What the Hall of Fame shows

Each entry in the Hall of Fame displays:

- **Token name, symbol, and image** — the token's identity as set by its creator.
- **Graduation timestamp** — the exact block time at which the token graduated.
- **Total miners** — how many unique wallets participated in the mining phase.
- **Mining duration** — how long it took from token creation to graduation.
- **Initial pool depth** — the USDC and token amounts that seeded the AMM at graduation.
- **Top miners** — the wallets that mined the most tokens during the mining phase.

### Why it matters

A token in the Hall of Fame means the community believed in it enough to fully mine it within the launch window. It is a signal of genuine community traction — not just a creator's ambition. Tokens that failed to graduate within the window are not listed.

<Tip>
  If you mined a token that appears in the Hall of Fame, your wallet address is permanently recorded in that token's transaction history on-chain. Your contribution is immutable.
</Tip>

## Access the Leaderboard and Hall of Fame

Both features are accessible from the ACTFUN homepage:

- Click **Leaderboard** in the top navigation bar to view the global miner rankings.
- Click **Hall of Fame** in the top navigation bar to browse graduated tokens.
